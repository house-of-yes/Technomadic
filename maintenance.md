## Quick Inspection Checklist
- [ ] Cold start within 5s (no blue/white smoke)
- [ ] Oil pressure ≥ 40 psi cold / ≥ 25 psi hot idle
- [ ] Coolant temp steady **190–210 °F** under load
- [ ] Exhaust manifolds (no tick, no missing bolts)
- [ ] Fuel: no seep at TBI base • pressure **11–13 psi**
- [ ] Ignition: clean cap/rotor, labeled wires 1–8
- [ ] Vacuum lines intact • idle vacuum ≥ **18 inHg**
- [ ] Battery 12.6V off / 13.8–14.4V running • grounds clean
- [ ] Belts quiet • fan clutch engages
- [ ] ATF pink/clear • smooth 1–2–3–OD shifts • no reverse shudder
- [ ] Brakes straight • steering play acceptable
- [ ] **House:** generator carries load, fridge cools (LP/120V), furnace & water heater fire, pump holds pressure, converter fan OK

## Service Intervals (Motorhome Use)
- **Oil & filter:** every **3–4k mi** or yearly  
- **Transmission fluid/filter (4L80E):** every **24k mi / 2 yrs**  
- **Coolant (green EG):** every **2 yrs**  
- **Belts & hoses:** every **3 yrs**  
- **Fuel filter:** every **12k mi / 2 yrs**  
- **In-tank fuel pump:** preventive replace @ **5 yrs** if history unknown

---

## Fluids & Specs (typical)
- **Engine oil:** 10W-30 or 15W-40 (high-zinc preferred)  
- **Trans:** Dexron III/Mercon (per 4L80E spec)  
- **Diff:** 75W-90 GL-5  
- **Cooling system:** 50/50 green ethylene glycol  
- **TBI fuel pressure:** **11–13 psi**

## Common Watch Items
- Exhaust manifold gaskets/bolts  
- Radiator efficiency & fan clutch  
- Distributor module (carry spare)  
- TBI base gasket & vacuum leaks  
- Grounds (engine ↔ frame strap)
